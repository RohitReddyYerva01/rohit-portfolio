About

From forecasting models to full-stack screens

I'm currently a Data Scientist / Machine Learning Engineer at Datanitiv, where I design forecasting systems — using LightGBM, Neural Prophet, DeepAR, and Prophet + XGBoost hybrids — that run in production with 90%+ accuracy.

What started as a model-building role expanded organically: I picked up .NET, MSSQL, and Angular from scratch to build the REST APIs, stored procedures, and UI screens that bring those models to end users — including authentication, new modules, and cross-team defect resolution.

Before that, I worked as a UI/UX designer on two startup teams, which is why I care as much about how a model's output looks and feels to a user as I do about its accuracy. I hold an AWS Cloud Practitioner certification and graduated in B.Tech CSE (AI & ML) from the Institute of Aeronautical Engineering.
